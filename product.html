Overview

Dettol Antiseptic Liquid A house is not a home without a bottle of iconic Dettol Antiseptic Liquid. Dettol Antiseptic Liquid, non-poisonous and non-staining disinfectant can be used for first aid, personal care and even used around the home. This versatile product provides all-round family protection against germs and has been recommended by medical professionals for generations. For Medical Uses Can be used on cuts, bites, abrasions, and insect stings by diluting one Dettol Antiseptic Disinfectant Liquid capful in 250 ml of Water. For Hygiene Bathing - Add 1 to 2 capfuls in the bath for a hygienic and refreshing experience. Although a gentle antiseptic, do not use on for babies except on medical advice. For Household Use Laundry - Linens, napkins, handkerchiefs, etc. One capful in 500 ml of water Floors and Hard surfaces - 2 capfuls in a bucket of water Lavatories, sinks, drains, etc. - Use undiluted Dettol
